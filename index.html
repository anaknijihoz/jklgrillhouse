Our Services

Back To Zero

Eliminating car odours, germs and bacteria in one simple step. It works by creating a mist that envelops the entire interior of the vehicle with our proprietary formula.

Mirror Detailing

We offer an array of luxury services throughout the years with our teams passion for detailing & dedication to bringing the most quality automotive detailing experience.

Interior Detailing

We have the products and tools to help protect your interior from the damage inflicted primarily by the sun and dirt brought in from the outside--there are products here to protect your leather, vinyl, plastic, glass, carpet, and upholstery from fading, brittleness, cracking, peeling, and stains.

Exterior Detailing

To restore the car's original glossy surface finish. We have degreasers to break down dirt and soil, detailing clay for removing embedded contaminates, an array of waxes and polishes.

Engine Wash

We clean your car engine to identify problems that can affect your vehicle's performance. Plus, you can prevent dirt, gunk, and other debris from damaging your engine's appearance.

Gas Wash

We offer fuel or gas wash , inside of the engine with raw fuel, we wash-off that protective layer of oil. As a result, any unburnt fuel in the cylinders can cause, fuel wash. So, your left with a, wet mixture of raw fuel. Consequently, that literally “washes” the oil away.

Engine Detail

We clean the exterior of the engine and the engine compartment, and applying dressing to protect and beautify the engine. It's very easy to do, and requires no more than 45 minutes to an hour every couple of months.

11-11 Support

Our contact numbers are available from 11am to 11pm to assist you with your motorcycle or car problems.

Our Humble Beginning..

      Founded from the dreams of a group of friends, JKL Carwash and Grill was built and conceptualized to serve people who are always on the go.

      Situated in the heart of Barangay Pulung Cacutud, JKL Carwash and Grill aims to serve mouth-watering dishes inspired from traditional Korean Cuisine.
